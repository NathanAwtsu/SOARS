Vision and Mission

Vision

ACOMSS is the recognized academic organization of B.S. Computer Science students at Adamson University that provides services to the students who need academic help in their course and support the Computer Science Department in sustaining the integral development of members within the campus.

Mission

ACOMSS is committed to promoting and enhancing the skills and capabilities of its members by cultivating their knowledge and technical skills in the use and application of computers through various academic activities lined up throughout the school year. Also, ACOMSS would like to develop socially conscious and value-oriented students through various extra-curricular activities. Thus, ACOMSS would be a force motivating students to become physically, mentally, and spiritually mature individuals.
